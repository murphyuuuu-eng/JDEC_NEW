About Us

Jiangsu Jingdian Electric Co., Ltd (JDEC)

Jiangsu Jingdian Electric Co., Ltd (JDEC) was established on September 18, 2011 through the integration of three enterprises and is headquartered in Taizhou Modern Science and Technology Park, Jiangsu Province.

JDEC inherits deep expertise in electrical insulation components, complete equipment and communication technologies, forming full-chain manufacturing capability from core components to complete systems.

JDEC's business covers China, India, Ukraine, Laos, Russia, Ethiopia and other countries and regions.

JDEC adheres to the business philosophy that products reflect character, continuously improving R&D, design, testing and sustainable development capabilities.
